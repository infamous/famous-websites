Nested Layouts

Famo.us’ ready-made layouts are cross-compatible and fully nestable. Let’s look at a few ways to build more complex layouts by nesting pre-built layouts within one another.

Nested GridLayout

You can put a GridLayout within a GridLayout by including an instance of GridLayout in the sequence of renderables supplied to the outer GridLayout instance.
