Help

We built Famo.us to help everyone in the world build amazing applications as a community. If you need help, or are looking to offer help to others in the community, we have many resources available.

IRC channel

For quick, synchronous responses from community all-stars, IRC is the place for you. If you have any problems at all, we recommend you join our IRC channel (#famous on Freenode) for live help. Freenode maintains this getting started guide for those new to IRC.

Reporting a general bug or making a suggestion

Found a bug? Let us know at bugs@famo.us.

Have a new idea or suggestion? We are all over it, so email us at suggestions@famo.us.

Reporting bugs with a specific piece of the framework

If you have a specific issue with a piece of the framework and are comfortable engaging on GitHub, see the issues reporting section guide in the project itself.
